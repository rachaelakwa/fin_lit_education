What is Asset Management

An asset manager is an institution that grows or protects investor capital by converting it into a portfolio of investments. Asset managers have two main goals:

  • Protect wealth of client
  • Grow wealth of client

Different types of asset managers:

  • Hedge Fund: Investment portfolio is “hedged” to perform regardless of market direction
    • See Examples Here
  • Mutual Fund Balanced portfolio that mutualizes investments across different product
  • Index Fund : Invest in a combination of products that will track against a benchmark
  • Exchange Traded Fund: Very similar to index fund but shares can be traded in the open market
  • Private Equity: Investment portfolio contains private assets such as equity in early stage companies.
