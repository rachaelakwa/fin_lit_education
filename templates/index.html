About Us

FinLit was created recognizing the gap in knowledge on financial literacy. We are on a mission to bridge this gap through education and gamification. To make learning about finance engaging and accessible, FinLit incorporates innovative methods, including a Jeopardy game tailored specifically to financial education. This interactive game format transforms complex financial concepts into an enjoyable and competitive activity, allowing users to test their knowledge, learn new information, and reinforce their understanding in a dynamic way. By leveraging the familiar and fun structure of Jeopardy, FinLit ensures that users are not only informed but also entertained, making the learning process more effective and memorable.
